FAQ

Questions, answered.

Why is the website build free? What's the catch?+
No catch at all. We build your website free because your monthly hosting subscription is how we sustain the service. As long as you're a Lauvra customer, your website is live, maintained, and fully supported. It's a partnership — we invest in building your online presence, you invest in a plan that keeps it running.
What does "domain included" mean on Scale and Elite?+
On the Scale plan your .com domain is completely free in year 1. From year 2 onward renewal is $12/year — we remind you 60 days ahead. On the Elite plan your domain is free for the first 2 years, then $12/year from year 3. Launch and Grow plans don’t include a domain, but you can add one for $12/year — the cheapest renewal rate available, cheaper than GoDaddy ($21/yr) and Squarespace ($20/yr).
What kind of email do I get?+
You get a fully professional yourname@yourbusiness.com email account — not a Gmail address. It works on all devices including mobile, includes a calendar, and looks completely professional to every client and customer you email.
How long until my website is live?+
Standard delivery is 3 business days from the moment you complete our onboarding questionnaire. Need it faster? Priority delivery gets you live in 24 hours, and our Emergency same-day service delivers within 8 hours. Both are available as add-ons at checkout.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?+
Yes, at any time. Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. No penalties, no awkward conversations.
Do I need any technical knowledge?+
None at all. We handle everything — domain setup, hosting configuration, email setup, SSL installation, and Google Business Profile verification. You just tell us about your business and we take it from there.
